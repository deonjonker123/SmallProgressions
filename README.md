# Small Progressions

**Quality-of-life and progression mod for Minecraft 1.21.1 (NeoForge)**  
_Heavily inspired by Tiny Progressions_

## What This Mod Adds

### Automation

Growth Crystals speed up crops in a radius with three upgrade tiers. Cobblestone Generators produce cobblestone automatically across five tiers. Item Collectors pick up drops and dump them into chestsâ€”advanced version has filters and adjustable range. Lava Generators passively produce lava. Water Reservoirs give you infinite water in one block.

### Storage

Storage Barrels in four tiers (Copper/Iron/Gold/Diamond) with expanded capacity. Fluid Tanks hold large amounts of liquids across four tiers. Linen Sacks are portable storage that keeps contents when broken.

### Processing

Brick Furnaces smelt four times faster than vanilla furnaces.

### Progression

Four armor tiers: Steel, Emerald (jump boost), Dragon (flight + combat buffs), and Wither (powerful combat buffs). Matching tool sets for all tiers plus Paxels (pickaxe/axe/shovel combo) for every material including vanilla. Ender Ore lets you mine for ender pearls.

### Farming

New crops: blueberries, raspberries, blackberries, maloberries, cotton, and flax. Greenhouse Glass boosts growth in sunlight.

### Building

Colored Glowstone in all 16 colors plus glass variants. Asphalt blocks with stairs/slabs/walls. Reinforced Obsidian and Glass (explosion-proof, wither-proof). Marble, Slate, and Soul Sandstone with full variant sets. Thatch blocks. Hardened Stone variants. McFloaty Blocks let you place solid blocks in mid-air.

### Food

Expanded food options including pizza, bacon dishes, cooked eggs, special effect apples (Iron/Redstone/Emerald/Diamond grant temporary buffs), and berries.

### Utilities

Repair Totems passively fix items in your inventory. Lava Infused Stone acts as a permanent lava source. Sickle harvests straw from grass. Steel as a new material tier.
